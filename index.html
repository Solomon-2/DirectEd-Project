About Us

Praxis is a community-driven platform dedicated to preserving and sharing the art of craftsmanship. We connect master artisans with those eager to learn, repair, and create, ensuring that traditional skills are passed down through generations. The idea for Praxis was born from a simple, frustrating experience: trying to get a beloved pair of leather boots resolved and discovering the last local cobbler had retired. This personal search revealed a much larger problem: foundational craft skills are disappearing, leaving us reliant on a culture of disposable, mass-produced goods that fill our landfills. We saw an opportunity not just to mourn this loss, but to reverse it. Praxis was created to build a bridge between the wisdom of master craftspeople and a new generation eager to create, repair, and own things with meaning, ensuring these essential skills don't just survive, but thrive.

What do we do?

Praxis is a comprehensive ecosystem designed to combat trhowaway culture by reviving the world of skilled craft. We empower a new generation of artisans through three core offerings: "Trade-in-a-Box" kits that provide the tools and master-led training to learn a craft; a Certified Artisan Marketplace where they can sell their durable, high-quality goods; and a "Repair & Restoration" service that connects their skills with customers needing to mend cherished items. By seamlessly integratiing education, commerce, and service, Praxis creates a sustainable community built on the value of making, owning, and preserving things that last.
